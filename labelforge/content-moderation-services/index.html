Frequently Asked Questions

Online content moderation involves categorization, review and rating of content. It involves monitoring comments shared by readers on blogs, videos and photos on social media. It enables businesses to censor their content or ask someone to do it for them. Large enterprises like Facebook have a dedicated department to keep a tab on their online content.

There are three key challenges to Content Moderation:-

1. Content Volume: There is an enormous quantity of user generated content on social media which differs from one platform to another. However, the growth of UGC is the same so the moderation needs to take this into account at all times.

2. Content Variety: User generated content can be of various types like text, videos, photographs, voice chats, etc. Hence, a content moderation system should be able to deal with all types of content on the social media platform.

3. Content Context: The context of any content is necessary prior to moderation. It is imperative that the content moderation solution is able to deal with it without altering its meaning and people viewing it.

Content moderators ensure businesses and end-users are saved from damaging unsettling posts and offers that are made by trollers and scammers. They ensure that UGC is moderated as per brand’s guidelines and goals.

A good content moderator must me patient, curious and have integrity. A steady pace along with accuracy is important during content moderation. One might face several challenges but must remain integrated and focused on a single goal.
