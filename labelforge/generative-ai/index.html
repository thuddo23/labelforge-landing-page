FAQs

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that is capable of creating a wide range of content, including text, images, audio, and synthetic data.

The key advantages of Generative AI include:

1. It automates the content writing process, significantly reducing manual effort.

2. It streamlines tasks such as responding to emails, making them less time-consuming.

3. It can generate realistic representations of people.

4. It helps create coherent narratives and summarizes complex information effectively.

5. It simplifies the process of generating content that adheres to a specific style.

Some of the main challenges associated with Generative AI are:

1. It often struggles to identify the original source of content.

2. It faces difficulties in evaluating the bias present in the content sources.

3. Its realistic-sounding outputs can make it hard to detect inaccuracies.

4. It can be challenging to adjust and tune the system for new or evolving scenarios.

5. The generated results may sometimes be biased, prejudiced, or even inflammatory.
